CLASSIC ARCADE GAME

Rules

Game starts automatically

Use arrow keys to move up, left, right, down

Stone tiles are safe zone

Beware of deadly ladybugs on the water tiles

Game resets when you are hit by ladybugs or ladybugs hits you

You must reach the top safety zone by avoiding the ladybugs to score points
